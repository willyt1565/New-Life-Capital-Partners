Privacy Policy.

Last updated: May 23, 2026

New Life Capital Partners respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

1. Who We Are

New Life Capital Partners ("New Life Capital Partners," "we," "us," or "our") is a real estate holding company and the parent of a growing ecosystem of real estate businesses. This Privacy Policy applies to the website newlifecapitalpartners.com (the "Site"). It does not cover the separate websites operated by our affiliated companies, each of which maintains its own privacy practices.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide. When you submit our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the information you choose to give us — typically your name, email address, phone number, the subject of your inquiry, and any message content.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our Site and its service providers may automatically collect limited technical information when you visit, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and the dates and times of access. This is used to operate, secure, and improve the Site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you;
  • Provide, maintain, secure, and improve the Site;
  • Send newsletter content you have requested;
  • Understand how the Site is used through aggregate analytics; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Cookies & Analytics

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies, including web analytics tools such as Google Analytics, to understand site traffic and usage in aggregate. These tools may set cookies on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; doing so may affect certain features of the Site.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on trusted third parties to operate the Site. These providers process data only as needed to perform their services and under their own privacy policies:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers contact-form submissions;
  • Beehiiv — manages newsletter subscriptions;
  • Google Analytics — provides aggregate website analytics;
  • GitHub Pages — hosts and serves the Site.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the service providers described above, with companies within the New Life Capital Partners ecosystem where relevant to your inquiry, or where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of New Life Capital Partners or others.

7. Data Retention

We retain the information you submit for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, provide requested communications, and meet legal or business requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Choices & Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided to us, and you may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any newsletter email. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information submitted through the Site. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for a general business audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. External Links

The Site links to other websites, including those of our affiliated companies and third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to info@newlifecapitalpartners.com or by phone at +1 (225) 571-9592.
