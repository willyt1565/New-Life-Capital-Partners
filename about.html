About

A Firm Built Deliberately.

New Life Capital Partners is a real estate holding company — and the parent of a growing ecosystem of property businesses built for the long term.

Our Story

A beginning, by design.

New Life Capital Partners did not begin with a building. It began with a conviction.

That conviction is simple: real estate, approached with discipline and genuine care, creates lasting value — for the communities it touches, the residents who live in it, and the partners who build alongside it. Everything we do is an expression of that single idea.

We are newly launched, and we regard that as a strength rather than a limitation. It means every company in our ecosystem, every process, and every standard has been chosen on purpose — built in this era, for this era, without inherited compromise. A deliberate beginning is not a small thing. It is the foundation everything else compounds on.

Today, New Life Capital Partners is the parent company behind two operating businesses — High Hopes Home Buyers, our property acquisition company, and Newera Property Management, our management company — with more to come. The name New Life is intentional: every property we touch is an opportunity for renewal, and every company we build is designed to endure.

Thomas Lawhun
Chief Executive Officer

Thomas Lawhun leads New Life Capital Partners. A Texas-based real estate problem-solver, he has been helping homeowners find real solutions since 2020 — work that grew into the multi-company ecosystem the firm operates today. Before real estate, Thomas spent nearly two decades in law enforcement, and he carried every value from that career into this one: integrity, transparency, and a calm, methodical approach to solving difficult problems. He guides the firm and its companies, High Hopes Home Buyers and Newera Property Management, with patience, accountability, and a long-term view.
